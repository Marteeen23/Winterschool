About Us

The Winter School of AI and Robotics is a groundbreaking venture by Technology Robotix Society in association with Centre for Artificial Intelligence, IIT Kharagpur and sponsored by IEEE Student Branch IIT Kharagpur. As a school focused on real-life applicatons, aided by a plethora of problem statements, it develops the firsthand skills of students and prepares them for industrial usage of advanced concepts in three core verticals.


Every year, the Winter School spanned across the first half of December, but this year due to the pandemic, it will be conducted during the weekends, in March. The School will be divided into 3 core verticals namely Image Processing, Autonomous Robotics, and Machine Learning. While Image Processing and Autonomous Robotics are for both first and second years, Machine Learning is exclusively for second years. Experts on these verticals from around the world will also be giving guest lectures on hot topics in today's industry!
